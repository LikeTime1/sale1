"I can't find any fresh tasty ice teas!"
Which is why I started making my grandpa's cold brew tea recipe.

The recipe is made with simple healthy ingredients:

REAL TEA
Always whole leaf teas, absolutely no artificial flavours and colours

PURIFIED WATER
Vancouver's best alpine water purified with activated charcoal

LOTS OF TIME
18-24 patient hours. All good things come to those who wait. 

Never any preservatives or sweeteners.

 

WHAT IS COLD BREW TEA? 

Regular bottled teas are quickly brewed with hot water first then cooled down with addition of preservatives and sugar for long shelf life.

Cold brew tea uses cold water to slowly brew for 18-24 hours in cool temperature. This method showcases unique raw and real tastes of tea at its best. Cold brew produces minimal caffeine and highlights the natural smooth and sweet flavour profiles that doesn't need sugar and milk to taste awesome!

Oollo cold brew teas are simplistic healthy and delicious ice teas that fits the modern wellness lifestyle. The perfect cold brew tea.
